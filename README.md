# EasyJobs

EasyJobs was created because, let's be honest, the modern job search has become an exhausting, hands-on chore. Submitting resumes manually to hundreds of portals is a tedious, repetitive motion that drains candidates of their time, leaving them begging for relief. 

Our mission is simple: to handle the friction of the job search for you. We provide a platform that gets down to business, takes the heavy load off your hands, and performs the search to completion—leaving both you and matching startups fully satisfied.

---

## How We Plan to Help You Get Hired

### 🚀 Direct, Frictionless Redirections
No more navigating confusing applicant tracking systems or filling out identical forms 50 times a day. EasyJobs curates high-growth startup roles and links you directly to the source, reducing the time from discovery to submission to a single click.

### 📄 Intelligent Profile Compacting
Large, unoptimized PDF resumes slow down recruiters and fail automated ATS checks. Our built-in simulator automatically downsamples and optimizes your resume payload, ensuring your profile is parsed cleanly and matches startup job parameters instantly.

### 🛡️ Privacy First
Your personal contact details and data are safe. Since everything is encrypted in transit and at rest, we keep your personal details secure from third-party data harvesters. If we don't store raw, unencrypted files in the backend, they are 100% safe with us.

### 🎯 Live Matching Indicators
As soon as your profile is configured, our active directory evaluates matching startups. The listings feed dynamically flags which roles match your exact background and preferences, allowing you to prioritize roles that have the highest probability of hiring you.
